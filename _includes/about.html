About

ShenzEd is a Professional Learning Network for the International School sector in Shenzhen, China. Members of the network come together to share exemplary practices in a collegial environment with the goal of collaboratively enriching pedagogical repertoires.

Professional Development

Have the opportunity to be exposed to new, creative ideas and reflect upon and review your teaching practice.

Collaboration

Meet with like-minded educators to discuss, explore, inquire into and gain exposure to new ideas and topics that are relevant to your teaching.

Networking

With so many international educators in the area, ShenzEd aims to connect educators from across the city.
